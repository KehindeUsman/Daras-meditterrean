    ICE CREAM

    Handmade,
    delicious ice cream
    creations

    With spring we start making ice cream and continue with joy and love until autumn. Our ice cream is made by hand according to a traditional recipe and uses local ingredients. Each ingredient is carefully selected to ensure the highest quality. The milk we use is homemade and fresh from local producers to guarantee full flavor and enjoyment for discerning palates.
